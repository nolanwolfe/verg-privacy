# Privacy Policy

Verg respects your privacy.

Verg does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.
All journal entries and content are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers.

Verg does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection tools.

If you choose to purchase a premium subscription, payment is processed securely by Apple via the App Store.
Verg does not receive or store payment information.

You may restore purchases at any time using the “Restore Purchases” option in the app.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via app settings or at:
support@verg.app
